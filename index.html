Launch a Real Startup in One Summer

Focusing on the core concepts of the Lean Canvas and Customer Validation, the Lindenwood Launch summer camp provides an immersive experience for high school students to learn entrepreneurship fundamentals.

Validate Your Idea

Students will vote on the best pitch ideas and teams will form to collaborate and execute on those ideas.

Learn how to use innovative tools like the Business Model Canvas and "Get out of the building!" to validate your product ideas.

Read More

Create A Prototype

Your team will create a Lo-Fi minimum viable product or bootstrap a website to demonstrate your idea.

Beta test your product with real customers and validate (or Invalidate?) your assumptions and refine what you've learned.

Read More

Pitch Your Business

Pitch your product, validated learnings and refined idea to a panel of Mentors, Entrepreneteurs and potential Investors.

Compete for bragging rights (or perhaps something more substantial?) and launch your business.
